About United In One

Unity creates brighter futures.

We are a global foundation committed to walking with underserved communities through education, health, clean water, and practical support that restores dignity and hope.

Our Story

Some journeys are planned. Ours wasn’t.

A simple layover in Kenya led to an unplanned visit to a local school, and in that classroom, everything shifted. We saw children ready to learn, teachers doing their best with too little, and a need we couldn’t walk away from. What began as one visit became a promise, and that promise became United in One.

That feeling has never left us. It’s what carried us into five countries and hundreds of children’s lives. But a promise made once isn’t enough. It has to be renewed, community by community, child by child.

That’s where you come in. Every person who gives, volunteers, or simply shares our story is renewing that same promise we made in one small classroom. You don’t have to have a layover in Kenya to feel the pull we felt that day, you just have to say yes to it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help address these critical challenges by supplying schools with essential school supplies providing wells for clean water, and providing basic hygiene and first aid kits to those in need.

Our Vision

A world united. We see communities thriving as diversity becomes our strength, and collaboration drives progress. Through unity, we’re building a future where every person has the chance to rise and shine.

What if the world came together as one?

What if differences became our strength and collaboration our greatest power? At United in One Global Foundation, we believe this dream is possible, and we’re working every day to make it a reality.

Health

Healthy Futures

Team Powered By Generosity

Different Backgrounds, One Vision

We come from different cultures, speak different languages, and bring our own unique talents to the table. But what truly unites us is our shared belief in the power of connection and collaboration. Together, we’re breaking boundaries and combining ideas to tackle global challenges in meaningful ways.

Driven by Passion

For us, this isn’t just work, it’s a calling. Every member of our board volunteers their time without pay, this mission means more to us than a paycheck ever could. When you donate, your dollars go straight to the mission and the communities and children who need them most.

Whether we are working alongside communities, planning impactful projects, or brainstorming bold ideas, we are driven by one thing: making a real difference. Every member of our team puts their heart into what they do, because we believe that change starts with compassion and action.

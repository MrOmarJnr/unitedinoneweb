Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find the answer here — about our mission, the donation process, or volunteering opportunities.

What is your mission?

Our mission is to empower underserved communities by addressing their most urgent needs, fostering long-term sustainability, and helping create opportunities for a better future.

How do you choose the communities you serve?

We carefully assess areas where the need is greatest, often partnering with local leaders and organizations to identify underserved communities that can benefit the most from our efforts.

What is included in the hygiene and first aid kits?

Our kits include necessities like soap, toothpaste, toothbrushes, sanitary pads, bandages, antiseptics, and other basic but critical supplies that many people in these communities cannot access easily.

How do clean water wells improve lives?

By providing access to clean water, we help reduce waterborne illnesses, improve sanitation, and free up time for education and employment, especially for women and children who would otherwise spend hours collecting water.

How can I donate to United in One Global Foundation?

You can donate directly through our website. We accept one-time contributions, recurring donations, and other forms of support. Every dollar helps us extend our impact!

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. As a 501(c)(3) organization, all donations to United in One Global Foundation are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

How is my donation used?

Your contribution goes directly toward supporting our programs, including building schools, drilling wells, and providing hygiene and first aid kits.

Can I volunteer with United in One Global Foundation?

Yes! We’re always looking for passionate individuals to join us in making an impact. Opportunities range from helping with fundraising, organizing supplies, and participating in certain on-the-ground projects.

Are you affiliated with any political or religious organizations?

No. United in One Global Foundation is independent and nonpartisan. Our work is driven by a commitment to support underserved communities, regardless of their political or religious affiliations, in a manner that aligns with international laws and humanitarian principles.

How can I keep track of the progress of your projects?

We believe in transparency. You can stay updated through our social media, where we regularly post updates, photos, and impact stories, as well as our email newsletter.

At United in One, we’re building a brighter future, one project at a time. Whether you’re donating, volunteering or spreading our message, you’re making a world of difference.

Grants for Good Program

Powering progress through community grants.

Grants for Good supports nonprofit partners and local initiatives creating measurable impact in education, health, clean water, and community development.

Contribute Apply / Partner
United for a Better Tomorrow

Small grants can unlock big community change.

We believe local leaders and organizations are some of the most powerful agents of change. Grants for Good helps put resources in the hands of people already serving their communities.

By pooling donations and awarding focused grants, United In One helps partners deepen impact, strengthen equity, and deliver lasting change where it is needed most.

How It Works

A simple process for meaningful impact.

01

You Give

Donors contribute throughout the year into one shared fund dedicated to community impact.

02

We Grant

United In One reviews opportunities and awards grants to mission-aligned nonprofit partners.

03

Communities Grow

Selected partners use the support to expand education, health, water, or community programs.

Grant Questions

Eligibility & selection criteria.

Mission-aligned nonprofits, community groups, schools, and local partners working in education, health, clean water, or community development may be considered.

Applications and opportunities are reviewed based on mission fit, community need, transparency, feasibility, and measurable impact.

The fund does not support personal expenses, for-profit ventures, partisan political activity, or projects that do not align with United In One’s mission and values.

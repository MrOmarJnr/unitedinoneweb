Is This Form For You? 🌍

This form is designed for school administrators, community leaders, or nonprofit leaders leading efforts to improve lives in underserved communities across Africa.

If your school, village, or organization needs support in areas like education or health & wellness, we want to hear from you. Share your community's story and needs so our team can explore how United In One Global Foundation can best support you. This form takes approximately 10–15 minutes to complete — be as detailed as possible, the more we know, the better we can serve you.

  • All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.
  • You may upload supporting documents such as a needs assessment or school registration letter (optional but helpful).
  • Once submitted, a member of our team will follow up with you within 5–7 business days.
